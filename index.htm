      Overview

      Located within 400 metres of Hoxton Square, Sleepy Joes Aparthotel London includes 8 rooms with views of the city. The aparthotel also provides Wi-Fi available throughout the property.

      Location

      Museum of the Home is 6 minutes' stroll away, while Hoxton train station is a short walk to this property. Guests can visit Float Hub, which is 5 minutes' walk away, and the unmissable Tower of London which is 2.5 km from the hotel. Boxpark Shoreditch is 14 minutes' walk away, and Old Street is within walking distance of the accommodation.

      For those travelling from afar, London City airport is 25 minutes' drive away.

      Rooms

      The rooms include coffee/tea making equipment, along with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels for your convenience. Furnished with a sofa, they also have soundproof windows. Bathroom amenities include a walk-in shower and a separate toilet, along with comforts such as hair dryers and bath sheets.

      Eat & Drink

      Within a short walking distance of this London hotel, Olive serves Italian dishes.
