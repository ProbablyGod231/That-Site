FAQs

Questions you asked us most

What time is check-in & out?

Your stay at Sleepy Joe’s can begin from 15:00 onwards, while check-out time is at 10:00. If you require a different check-in or check-out time, please let us know and we will do our best to accommodate you. In case we are unable to do so, we have lockers available on-site where you can store your luggage.

I am arriving early, can I store my luggage with you?

Yes you can! We have large lockers on site.

How does self-check-in work?

You will receive all necessary information one day before check-in to make this process seamless. Our apartments feature state-of-the-art door locks to ensure you are safe and don’t need to worry about keys or losing a room key.
