FAQs

Everything you want to know

Good to know

What time is check-in & out?

Your stay at Sleepy Joe’s can begin from 15:00 onwards, while check-out time is at 10:00. If you require a different check-in or check-out time, please let us know and we will do our best to accommodate you. In case we are unable to do so, we have lockers available on-site where you can store your luggage.

I'm arriving early, can I store my luggage with you?

Yes you can! We have large lockers on site.

It's pet-friendly; what are the conditions?

We love our four legged friends at Sleepy Joe’s. There will be no charge for your dog to stay in the apartment. If the apartment is damaged or super messy, there will be a fee for cleaning or repair costs at check-out. Details must be provided before booking the apartment.

Is there a minimum length of stay?

There is no minimum stay; you can stay for 1 night or 1 month

Arrival, departure & stay

How does self-check-in work?

You will receive all necessary information one day before check-in to make this process seamless. Our apartments feature state-of-the-art door locks to ensure you are safe and don’t need to worry about keys or losing a room key.

How often does my apartment get cleaned?

This is upon request for longer stays; however, all apartments feature washing machines and dryers should you need them.

Is there space to accommodate extra guests?

Both our one and two-bedroom apartments have a sofa bed that can accommodate one or two extra guests. Please make sure to stipulate this when booking so we can ensure everything is prepared.

I have a small child, what do I need to arrange?

We can place a baby cot within your apartment upon request. Please mention this during booking to ensure it’s all prepared for check-in.

Mobility & accessibility

Are the apartments wheelchair accessible?

The building of Sleepy Joe’s has a lift and is wheelchair accessible. Please do not hesitate to contact us if you require further information.

Are the apartments wheelchair accessible?

The building of Sleepy Joes offers a lift and is therefore accessible for disabled people. However, please do not hesitate to contact our reservations department for further information in this area.

Is parking available nearby?

Unfortunately, we do not offer parking on the premises, but street parking is payable during certain hours. We suggest downloading the JustPark app to give you all the necessary information and tariffs.

My booking

I would like to book several apartments, how do I do that?

Via the online booking module you can book more than 1 apartment at the same time. Your booking is considered a group when you book 3 apartments or more. Do you want to make a group reservation or do you have any questions regarding this? Please contact us via welcome@sleepy-joes.co.uk

I would like to book several apartments, how do I do that?

You can book multiple apartments at the same time; however, if you have any questions regarding this, please get in touch with us at welcome@sleepyjoes.co.uk

How to change or edit my booking?

Changing or cancelling your reservation depends on the reservation you made. Our cancellation policies are quite strict, however, the best thing to do would be to send us an email at welcome@sleepyjoes.co.uk, if you have any requests.

What payment methods does Sleepy Joe's accept?

Reservations must be paid with a credit or debit card when booking. Please note that payment is taken in local currency (GBP) and may be subject to currency conversion fees from your bank.
